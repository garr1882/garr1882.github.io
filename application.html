Overview

Reach the end by guiding the ball down each level of the tower. Make sure to avoid the dark tiles, otherwise you will restart the round.

This game is relaxing, but it is important to keep attention to detail!!

How to Play

  • Click to start.
  • Then, click and hold your mouse when you want to move the ball
  • Make sure you don't land on the black tiles!

Tip: you can hold down the mouse to move fast

Click C to change camera view.
